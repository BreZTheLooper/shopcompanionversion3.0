Shop Companion collects basic personal information (name, email, phone) to facilitate transactions. Data is stored locally and not sold to third parties. We comply with the Philippine Data Privacy Act (RA 10173).

While we strive for accuracy, product pricing and availability are ultimately determined at the cashier's system.

5. Rewards Program

Customers earn 1 point per ₱10 spent. Points may be redeemed for discounts subject to availability. Points have no cash value and are non-transferable.

6. Multi-Store Access

One account works across all stores. However, your cart is store-specific and cannot mix items across stores. When switching stores, your cart for that store is loaded separately.

Shop Companion serves as a facilitating system. All disputes must be resolved directly with the retailer. The generated QR code is a request for checkout, not a payment confirmation.
